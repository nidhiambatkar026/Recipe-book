COCONUT MILK RICE DELIGHT

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup jasmine rice (or any long-grain rice)
  2. 1 cup coconut milk
  3. 1 cup water
  4. 2 tablespoons sugar (optional, for a sweeter taste)
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional, for added flavor)
  7. 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional, for garnish)
  8. Fresh fruit (mango, pineapple, or berries) for serving (optional)
