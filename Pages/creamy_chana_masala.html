CREAMY CHANA MASALA

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups chickpeas (garbanzo beans), soaked overnight or 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  2. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  3. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  4. 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  6. 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped or 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  7. 1-2 green chilies, chopped (optional, for extra heat)
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  9. Salt to taste
  10. Lemon wedges (for serving)
