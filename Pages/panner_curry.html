PANEER CURRY DHABA SYTLE

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  2. 250 grams paneer
  3. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  4. 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped or 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  5. 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  7. 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped or 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  8. 1-2 green chilies, chopped (optional, for extra heat)
  9. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
